Global warming is a topic that affects everyone on earth.

No matter your race, gender, religion or age, global warming affects all of us and could seriously impact the habitability of our planet.
If things countinue to escalate as they are, the climate will soon not be hospitable for human life, and there is nowhere else for us to go.
While the leading cause of global warming is

industrialization and urbanization

, there is still plenty the common household can do to reduce their

carbon footprint

and help the enviroment.
